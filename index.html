Red Wine
Red wine is a type of wine made from dark-colored grape varieties. Its deep flavors often include notes of berries, plums, and spices, with varying levels of tannins and acidity. Red wine is commonly enjoyed with meals and is known for its potential health benefits in moderation.

Blue Lagoon
The Blue Lagoon is a strikingly vibrant cocktail known for its electric blue color, made with vodka, blue curaçao, and lemonade. It’s a refreshing, citrusy drink that's perfect for summer. Often garnished with a slice of lemon or cherry, the Blue Lagoon is both visually stunning and deliciously crisp.

Raspberry Martini
A Raspberry Martini is a fruity and elegant cocktail that combines the tartness of fresh raspberries with the smoothness of vodka. Typically made with raspberry liqueur, fresh lemon juice, and a splash of simple syrup, this martini is shaken over ice and served in a chilled glass, often garnished with a raspberry.

Salads

margarita
A Strawberry Margarita is a refreshing and fruity cocktail made with tequila, fresh strawberries, lime juice, and triple sec.
