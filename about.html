About Us

Catch Up Bangalore, located in the vibrant HSR Layout Sector 6, is a popular dining spot known for its eclectic menu featuring a mix of Chinese, North Indian, and Continental cuisines. The restaurant offers a contemporary dining experience with a warm and stylish ambiance, making it an ideal place for both casual lunches and romantic dinners. Catch Up has been serving the Bangalore community for several years, becoming a favorite spot for locals and visitors alike due to its unique fusion dishes and excellent service. The restaurant is especially praised for its diverse menu, which includes everything from classic North Indian curries to flavorful Asian-inspired dishes. In addition to its regular dining services, Catch Up also provides a takeaway option, catering to the busy urban crowd who prefer enjoying their meals at home. The interior of the restaurant is designed to be cozy yet modern, making it a comfortable space to relax and enjoy a meal with friends or family.

Catch Up is making an exciting comeback in HSR Layout, Bangalore, with an all-new experience featuring a Brew House Pub, a Fine Dine section, and a Rooftop Lounge. After the challenges of the pandemic, this beloved spot is set to become a go-to destination for great food, drinks, and vibrant ambiance. With a fresh vibe, innovative menus, and stunning rooftop views, Catch Up promises to offer a perfect blend of relaxation and entertainment, making it a must-visit for those looking to unwind and socialize in style.
